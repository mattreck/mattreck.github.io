About Wheatless

The Wheatless Wonder was created by Marty when he decided that he wanted to make delicious Italian foods for those who were Gluten Free. With Gluten Free becoming a norm in today's society, Marty decided to create a restaurant that makes quality pastries and an amazing lunch and dinner menu. Stop on by and enjoy Gluten Free pastries and entrees specially prepared for you!

Discover Our Best Chefs

When we opened The Wheatless Wonder, we hired the best Executive Chef available in New York to create the finest bakery and restaurant items. Our team is the most experienced in New York in Gluten Free pastries and entrees that you are sure to fall in love with. Our Head Chef Joseph Mauro is on a mission to craft the best Italian Gluten Free pastries, breads, and full meals in New York. We hope you enjoy our food, and if you have any issues you can always call us directly and speak with our head Chef or Owner.

Joseph Mauro
Head Chef

After years of research, including studies and asking what gluten-free people would like to eat and what they were missing, Chef Joseph set out to create delectable Gluten Free bakery items and full course meals. When Marty decided to open a gluten-free Italian restaurant and bakery, Joseph Mauro was one of the top choices for selection to be Head Chef of the Restaurant. Joseph knew a gluten-free Italian menu does not have to miss out on flavor, texture, or delicious options. Joseph has spent the last 7 years doing research on Gluten Free foods, and 6 of those years as Head Chef in restaurants and bakeries across the nation.

"Creating 100% Gluten Free foods that aren't cookie cutter is my mission, and if I do say so myself, I am pretty good at it."
