About Us

The Wheatless Wonder was created by Marty when he decided that he wanted to make delicious Italian foods for those who were gluten free. With Gluten Free becoming a norm in today's society, Marty decided to create a restaurant that makes quality pastries and an amazing lunch and dinner menu. Stop on by and enjoy Gluten Free pastries and entrees specially prepared for you!
